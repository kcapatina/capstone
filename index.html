SKYROCKET OF SUCCESS

People who dream for a long time will eventually resemble their dreams. ~ RIIZE Anton

Due to an overwhelming demand, WE LITTLE RIIZE “RIIZE PARK”™ will be opening international locations across multiple cities and countries! Initially operating as a pop-up store in Seoul, many fans missed the opportunity to purchase exclusive park merchandise and partcipate in themed rides, instructions voiced by the RIIZE members themselves.

After reaching out to SM Entertainment,—the company RIIZE is under— it was confirmed that the global success brought attention to the group. The positive reactions of attendees encouraged the construction of the parks, and has since been greenlit. While it's an ambitious project, fans are excited to be able to walk into a world decorated by WE LITTLE RIIZE mascots and to keep an eye out for easter eggs that the members have hidden within the designs of the theme park. As Anton once stated, people who dream for a long time will eventually resemble their dreams. It comes as a surprise to the group as well, with the unexpected rise in popularity. Specifics will be brought at a later date, with the project starting in 2030.
