The appeal of WE LITTLE RIIZE doesn’t stop there. These characters have captured the hearts of the general public beyond just the group’s fans. In particular, “DDOLBYEONG”—modeled after RIIZE member SOHEE—sparked countless memes across various communities immediately after its release. The character’s unique expressions and high similarity to SOHEE became a hot topic, causing demand to skyrocket. Through its cute, friendly designs, WE LITTLE RIIZE has naturally introduced RIIZE to non-fans.

The popularity of WE LITTLE RIIZE, beloved by both fans and the public, continues to grow. Character merchandise sells out instantly upon release, and the approximately 5,400-square-foot pop-up store in Seoul held from Apr. 4 to Apr. 20 saw a constant stream of visitors. The pop-up store featured DIY character-patch T-shirt making, custom member-character photocard and name-sticker stations and four-shot photo booths—each drawing enthusiastic responses.
