Visiting Riize Park

On March 18 at midnight, RIIZE rolled out new teaser photos through social media and anticipated a new project. In the teasers, the members are dressed in the adorable mascot dolls that the members designed themselves. The photos were shared with a short explanation about each mascot with the caption "COMING SOON." So stay tuned for the upcoming RIIZE content!

TV animation and comic book characters are now playing active roles in our daily lives. Characters that have emerged on the music industry stage and social media are rapidly spreading and capturing the public’s affection.

WE LITTLE RIIZE offers fans a diverse entertainment experience. Fans cherish these characters because the members designed them personally, and each character so perfectly reflects the members’ appearance and personality that fans see WE LITTLE RIIZE as extensions of RIIZE. As a result, merchandise and content featuring these characters have become “must-collect” and “must-consume” items. Furthermore, fans playfully communicate with the artists by using WE LITTLE RIIZE as a focal point, making it an effective communication channel that has earned passionate support.
